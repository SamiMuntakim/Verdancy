Common questions

My scan says "Unknown Plant"

That's Verdancy being honest rather than guessing. Retake the photo in good light, close to the leaves, with as little background clutter as possible.

Managing or cancelling my subscription

Subscriptions are handled by Apple: Settings app → your name → Subscriptions → Verdancy, or the Manage subscription link in the app's Settings tab. To restore a purchase on a new device, use Restore purchases in Settings.

Care reminders aren't showing

Check that notifications are allowed for Verdancy in iOS Settings, and that the in-app Care reminders toggle is on. Reminders schedule from each plant's care cadence.

Deleting my account

Settings → Account → Delete account. This permanently removes your data — it can't be undone.
