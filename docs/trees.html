How the pledge works

  • 10 trees per subscriber, staggered across the first subscription year.
  • +1 tree per milestone you reach in the app (first plant, fifth plant, a friend joining from your invite, …).

Keeping it honest

A tree counter is only worth something if it's real. Plantings are fulfilled in aggregate through an established reforestation partner (~$1 funds one tree planted), and we will publish the partner's name and verification receipts on this page with every batch. Until the first batch is confirmed, the in-app number represents pledged trees.
