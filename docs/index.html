Fair questions.

Why isn't it free?

Free plant apps pay for themselves with ads and your data. Verdancy has neither — your subscription pays for the AI that identifies your plants, and for actual trees going into actual ground. Your first scan is free so you can judge the care card before paying anything.

What if it can't identify my plant?

Then it tells you exactly that. You'll get "Unknown Plant" and a tip to retake the photo in better light — never an invented schedule. Your free allowance includes retake headroom, so a blurry first shot doesn't cost you your free scan.

Are the trees real?

Yes — planted in aggregate through an established reforestation partner (roughly $1 funds one tree). We publish the partner and verification receipts with every batch on the forest page. Until a batch is confirmed, your in-app count is a pledge, and we say so.

How hard is it to cancel?

Two taps. Subscriptions are handled entirely by Apple — Settings → your name → Subscriptions. The trial won't charge you if you cancel before day seven.
